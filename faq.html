Shoppie - FAQs

What is Shoppie?
⇒ Shoppie is an online shopping platform that allows users to browse and purchase a wide variety of products.
What payment methods are accepted
⇒ We currently only accept Cards for payment
How do i apply for a Refund
⇒ To apply for refund please contact the Customer Support
Are the products Genuine
⇒ Yes all the products sold on our website are from Genuine sellers
How do i check my Delivery status?
⇒ You can check your delivery status by logging into your account and visiting the 'Orders' section.
What if I am unavailable for pickup for the delivery?
⇒ If you are unavailable for pickup, you can reschedule the delivery through your account or contact customer support for assistance.
What if i recieved a Damaged Product?
⇒ If you receive a damaged product, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.
